# Cheese app 

## Overview
A cheese discovery and tracking app that displays photos of different cheese varieties and allows users to log their tasting experiences. The app helps cheese enthusiasts explore new types and maintain a personal tasting journal.

## Problem
cheese lovers struggle to remember which cheeses they've tried, what they liked about them, and discover new varieties to taste next

## Target User
cheese enthusiasts, foodies, and culinary explorers who want to systematically discover and track different cheese varieties

## Features
- cheese photo gallery with variety information
- personal tasting log with ratings and notes
- cheese discovery recommendations
- search and filter by cheese type/origin
- wishlist for cheeses to try
